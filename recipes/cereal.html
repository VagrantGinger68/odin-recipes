Cereal

Description

A bowl full of cold milk with your favorite cereal inside of it.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cereal
  • Spoon

Steps

  1. Grab a bowl.
  2. Grab your favorite cereal.
  3. Pour 2 cups of your cereal into the bowl.
  4. Take the milk out of the fridge.
  5. Pour a cup of milk into the bowl. Try covering the cereal to make it all wet.
  6. Don't spill the milk please.
  7. Put the spoon into the bowl.
  8. Enjoy!
