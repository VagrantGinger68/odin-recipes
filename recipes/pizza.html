Pizza

Description

White dough stretched 14" in diameter. Tomato sauce spread all the way to the edge. 6 ounces of mozzarella cheese. With cripsy Pepperoni on top.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Thaw frozen dough overnight in the fridge.
  2. Stretch the thawed dough onto a 14" pan for a large pizza containing 10 slices.
  3. Allow the dough to sit for an hour to rise.
  4. Poke the dough with a fork to prevent air bubbles with it cooks in the oven.
  5. Spread a thin layer on sauce across the dough, leaving an inch gap around the edge for the crust.
  6. Put a layer of 20 Pepperoni's on top of the sauce.
  7. weigh out 6 ounces of mozzarella cheese (7.5 ounces if you want extra).
  8. Spread the cheese evenly over the dough.
  9. Bake for 7 minutes at 700 degrees fahrenheit.
  10. Let it cool for 5 minutes so you don't burn your mouth.
  11. Enjoy!
