Protein Shake

Description

Whey protein powder dissolved into a glass of soy milk

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Protein Powder
  • Chocolate Soy Milk
  • A Cup
  • A Spoon

Steps

  1. Grab the scoop that is included with your protein powder.
  2. Dump a scoop into your cup.
  3. Pour 200ml of Soy milk into your cup.
  4. Stir the milk until the protein powder is fully dissolved.
  5. Enjoy!
